My Approach

Teaching Philosophy

"Language learning should be personalised, practical, and enjoyable."

Every student has different goals — passing an exam, preparing for travel, advancing a career, or communicating confidently in daily life. My role is to help each student reach those goals in the most effective way possible.

I work closely with students to set clear learning objectives and create a realistic study plan based on their desired timeline. Because learning is not always a straight path, I regularly adjust lessons according to each student's progress, strengths, and challenges.

My lessons are flexible and student-centred, combining structured learning with real-world communication practice. I encourage students to actively participate, make mistakes without fear, and develop confidence. My ultimate goal is not only to improve language skills, but to make every student enjoy the journey and stay motivated throughout.
